Chocolate Fudge

Description

These brownies are to die for! They have been in the family dessert list for generations!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
  • ⅔ cup evaporated milk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup chopped nuts
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
